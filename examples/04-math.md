# Math (KaTeX)

Inline and display math. Back to the [index](README.md).

## Inline

Euler's identity, $e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$, is often called the most beautiful
equation. The mass–energy relation is $E = mc^2$.

## Display

The Gaussian integral:

$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2}\,dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$

The quadratic formula:

$$
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$

A matrix:

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 \\
0 & 1
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
x \\ y
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
x \\ y
\end{bmatrix}
$$

## Mixed with prose

The sum of the first $n$ natural numbers is $\sum_{k=1}^{n} k = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}$,
which you can prove by induction.
