# Footnotes

Footnotes collect references at the bottom. Back to the [index](README.md).

The honey badger is famously fearless[^badger] and will take on prey much
larger than itself. Octopuses, by contrast, have three hearts[^octo] and blue
blood.

You can also have inline-defined references[^long] with longer explanations.

[^badger]: It has been documented chasing away lions. Respect.
[^octo]: Two pump blood to the gills, one to the rest of the body.
[^long]: Footnotes can contain multiple sentences, `code`, and even
    [links](https://example.com). They're handy for citations without
    cluttering the main text.
